## 📈 Repository Contents

### 1. ACI 214R Statistical Strength Analyzer & Target Mix Optimizer
**File:** `5-Data-Driven Concrete Quality Control.ipynb`

This notebook serves as an advanced, zero-code computational engine designed for concrete technologists, QA/QC managers, and structural consultants. By implementing the rigorous statistical frameworks of **ACI 214R-11** and **ACI 318M**, it transforms raw historical site data into a strategic gold mine for mix optimization.

**Key Features:**
* **Overall vs. Within-Test Variation:** Automatically isolates production/material errors from laboratory testing errors (Ninja Lab precision).
* **Target Strength ($f'_{cr}$) Optimization:** Mathematically calculates the reduced required average strength based on your proven historical standard deviation.
* **Economic & Thermodynamic Impact:** Mathematically compress variance to safely reduce cement content (kg/m³), lowering the heat of hydration, mitigating thermal cracking, and maximizing profit.
* **CUSUM Control Charts:** Early detection system for shifts in aggregate sources or cement chemistry.
